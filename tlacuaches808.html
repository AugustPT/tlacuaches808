Step-by-step guide

How to find our hidden gem

We are tucked away from the main Waikīkī streets inside the tranquil Eaton Square courtyard. Follow these three visual steps from the outside directly to our storefront counter.

01

1. Front Entrance

Look for the large blue EATON SQUARE sign archway on Hobron Lane to enter the ground-floor shopping walkways.

02

2. Courtyard Walkway

Walk into the open-air center courtyard. You will spot the stone Tlacuaches sign straight ahead under the Post Office sign.

03

3. Storefront Counter

Arrive at Unit 115! Order signature birria tacos at our window, check out our daily chalkboards, and meet our chef.
